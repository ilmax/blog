Hello and welcome! My name is Massimiliano, I am a software engineer and cloud architect with a passion for all things tech. I was born and raised in Italy and I currently live and work in the Netherlands.

I have been working as a developer since 2008, building software across a wide range of industries and project types. I specialise in Azure, .NET and C#, with a particular interest in Infrastructure as Code and DevOps practices. I am also a strong believer in Cloud Native principles and enjoy working on projects that leverage cloud computing to deliver scalable, high-performing solutions.

In addition to my professional experience, I hold several certifications including Azure and Terraform, which I have worked hard to achieve and maintain.

Thank you for visiting — I hope you find something useful here, and feel free to reach out if you have any questions or just want to chat about tech.
